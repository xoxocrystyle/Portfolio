Who i am

I'm a web developer based in Orange County. I discovered my love for coding out of sheer curiosity. I am one to try new things and after seeing Covergirl’s Girls Who Code commercial, I wanted to take up a new skill. When I began to learn the languages I know now, I got knocked down but continuously got back up. Never have I ever been unsure about taking on this career path. I’m committed to this journey of constantly learning. Coding has pushed me to get out of my comfort zone. No matter what obstacle I face, I will always know that there will be solution to the problem and that I will always be supported by a wonderful community. I have a driving passion for programming that I can’t fathom. To keep up my curiosity, I’m always on Medium, Quora, watching tutorials online, or listening to podcasts (ask me which are my favorite!).

Outside of the coding world, I love to feed my other passions such as music, reading, and outdoors. Music keeps me motivated and I listen to different genres from pop punk, EDM, to R&B. When it comes to reading I love to read about self-improvement or fictional novels. Taking these two things outdoors is a recipe for a great adventure. I’ve been on multiple roadtrips and I love hiking. Listening to great music during the trek and taking a novel to relax at the end of trail is my way of relaxing.
