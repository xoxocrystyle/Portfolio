Live Face Off
by Crystal Navarro, Khaleel Younis, Shawn Levin, Paul Lee
Live Face Off is a web application where we let friends and family stay in touch through video chat and playing games. What sets our app apart from others is that we wanted to have an easy user interface and experience for people of all ages to use. Our app provides easy login and sign up through Facebook and Google. We then give our users the option to keep in touch by playing our built in game or play a game a through a video call.
