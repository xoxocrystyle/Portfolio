Beets & Eats
by Crystal Navarro, Kelcey Lorenzo, Andrea Wayte, Nick Quan, and Khaleel Younis
Beets and Eats is a web application that was part of a hackathon and built within two days. Our app plans your day or night according to which event you attend. You simply input your location and date. After hitting the submit button, the page is populated with plays, concerts, or live sports from TicketMaster. Once you choose your event, you are taken to a map that displays bars and restaurants near the venue.

Technologies used: HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap 4, Google API, Yelp API, Ticketmaster API, jQuery
